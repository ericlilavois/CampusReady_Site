We’ve launched Campus Ready Foundation

Eric and I started Campus Ready to help first-time college students cover the hidden costs that show up before classes begin—move-in essentials, travel, and meals before dining halls open—so they start with confidence and dignity, not debt.

What we do: Student Essentials Kits, travel support, and early-arrival meals for low-income students.

We’re a 501(c)(3). We funded our initial launch and have early support from Target. If this resonates, a quick share or intro to a counselor or college contact is a big help.
