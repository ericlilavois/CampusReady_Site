I'm writing to share great news that has my heart full – Eric and I officially launched the Campus Ready Foundation, and I couldn't be more excited about the difference we're going to make together.

The foundation was born from a moment we experienced, and you can likely imagine. While helping Max move into college, I was struck by all the hidden costs that stack up before classes even begin – travel, move-in essentials, and meals before school cateterials open. I found myself asking: how do families without resources cover these costs so their students can start college with confidence and dignity, not debt?

Campus Ready Foundation bridges the gap by providing Student Essentials Kits, travel support, and early-arrival meals to first-generation, low-income college students.

We formed a certified 501(c)(3) non-profit, funded the initial investment, received backing from partners like Target, and we're just getting started. What thrills me is the potential of setting up superstar students, who've already overcome so much to reach college, for success on day one.

I'd love for you to learn more about our mission, and if it speaks to you, please share it with others who might also care about helping students start strong.
