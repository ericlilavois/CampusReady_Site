Why partner with us

Measurable impact. A lean organization. A direct line to students.

Campus Ready Foundation is built to be a responsible steward of partner support. We are lean by design, with a direct line between partner contributions and student awards. Our application and selection process is transparent and rubric-based. Our reporting is specific: partners know how many students were served, what they received, and when.

For corporate partners, that specificity matters. It means your ESG or social impact reporting reflects real outcomes rather than general contributions. It means your employees can see — concretely — what their organization made possible.

Ways to partner

Every partnership is structured around outcomes — students served, awards provided, impact reported. Choose the path that fits your organization, or reach out to build something custom.

Ready One

$2,500

Fund one complete Campus Ready award — move-in essentials, travel to campus, early-arrival support, and a gift card for incidentals. One student arrives ready. Partners at this level are included in the Foundation's annual impact update.

Ready a Class

$25,000

Fund ten complete awards. Your contribution equips ten students at the start of their college journey. Includes logo recognition on the Foundation website, a detailed annual impact report, and optional social acknowledgment of your partnership.

Ready a Community

$100,000

Fund forty students across multiple campuses. This level of support creates meaningful scale — cohorts of students across institutions who begin their college experience with stability rather than stress. Includes elevated logo placement, an annual impact report, the option to name your cohort, and an optional volunteer touchpoint connecting your team to the Foundation's work.

In-Kind Support

Provide products or services that go directly to students — move-in essentials, transportation, meal support, or other contributions that map to what our grants cover. Acknowledgment is calibrated to the scope and nature of your contribution. Lyft is an example of what this looks like in practice.

Custom Partnership

Some of the most meaningful partnerships don't fit a tier. Regional cohorts, ERG-led giving campaigns, seasonal initiatives, multi-year commitments — if you have a vision for how your organization wants to show up, we'll build the structure around it. Recognition and reporting matched to your goals.
