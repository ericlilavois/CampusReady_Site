Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about giving to Campus Ready Foundation.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. Campus Ready Foundation is a 501(c)(3) public charity. EIN 39-2532572.

Where does my gift go?

To Campus Ready awards—move-in kits, travel, and first-week meals. Unless you restrict a gift, we use funds where needed most across these areas.

When do I get a receipt?

Immediately by email for online gifts. For checks and DAF grants, we acknowledge upon receipt. If you need a copy, email hello@campusready.foundation.

Can I give monthly?

Monthly giving is coming soon. For now, one-time gifts are available.

Other ways to give?

Yes—DAF, stock, wire/ACH, and check by mail.
Check by Mail: Payable to Campus Ready Foundation, 1895 Madrona Ave, Saint Helena, CA 94574.
