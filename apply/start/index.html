Am I eligible?

  • First-time college student
  • Enrolled full-time this year
  • Living on campus (res hall or campus-managed housing)

(No distance requirement.)

What you’ll need

  • Your contact info and school details
  • Home address (campus address if you have it)
  • A short household snapshot (basic info only)
  • Two essays (200–500 words each):
    • Why is this award meaningful to you?
    • Describe a challenge you experienced in applying and/or preparing for college.

What we provide

  • Move-in basics (bedding, towels, storage, supplies)
  • Travel support to campus
  • Early-arrival meals (meal cards) for your first days

Timeline (PT)

  • Applications open

  • Decisions announced

  • Awards provided

    July 15 – Aug 1

Tip: set a reminder for May 15.

How decisions are made

We look first at financial need, your readiness to thrive on campus, and alignment with our mission to help students start strong with dignity. Your answers help us understand your situation and goals.
