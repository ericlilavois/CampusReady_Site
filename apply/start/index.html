Who this is for

This grant is for first-time college students enrolling full-time and living on campus — no distance requirement, no minimum GPA, and no expectation of a perfect application.

If that's you, we want to hear from you.

Financial aid covers tuition. We cover what it doesn't: the costs that show up before your first class even starts.

What the grant covers

A grant that covers what financial aid doesn't.

Move-in essentials

Bedding, towels, storage, toiletries, and the supplies your college's checklist requires, so you arrive with what you actually need.

Travel to campus

Transportation to campus for you and a parent or guardian on move-in day, whether that's across the state or across the country.

Early-arrival support

Meal support and a $100 gift card for incidentals for those first days on campus, before dining services open and before you've found your footing.

The process

Step 1

You apply

Jan 1 – May 15. Takes about 20–30 minutes, and you can save your progress and return any time before the deadline.

Step 2

We review

Every application is read by our board with care. We look at your full picture — not just your numbers.

Step 3

Awards delivered July 15 – Aug 1, ahead of move-in day.

Before you begin

Here's what to have ready.

Your information

Contact info and the name and address of your college

Your expected start date and confirmation you'll live on campus

Basic household snapshot — income and number of people at home

FAFSA or CADAA completion status and your SAI, if you have it

Two short essays

Why this award is meaningful to you

200–300 words

A challenge you faced while applying or preparing for college

200–500 words

We want to get to know you — your situation, your goals, and what this opportunity would mean for you. Write honestly. That's what matters to us.

How we review

We look first at financial need, your readiness to thrive on campus, and how well our grant can make a real difference for you. We read every essay. Your answers help us understand who you are — not just what your numbers say.
